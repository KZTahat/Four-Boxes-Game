Instructions

Welcome to Four Boxes Game!

This fast-paced challenge will test your reflexes and attention to detail. Your goal is simple: click on the correct light box to increase your score. Here's how it works:

  • Starting the Game: The game kicks off after your first click. Get ready for an exciting sequence of events!
  • Clicking Mechanics: Each correct click turns off the lit box and randomly lights up one of the other three boxes. Your counter increases with every accurate click, so aim for precision!
  • Time Pressure: Don't let the clock run out! If you go more than 2 seconds without clicking, the light box will change randomly, and your counter will decrease by one. If the counter hits zero, it's game over.

How to Play:

  • Use your mouse, left click on Blue, right click on Yellow.
  • React quickly to keep the game going.
  • Watch out for the timer and make every click count!
